# Welcome to Walty

Walty is a free and open-source self-custodial wallet for EVM networks. Manage your crypto assets across Ethereum-compatible chains from a single interface with complete control over your private keys.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Self-Custodial" icon="shield-check">
    Your keys, your crypto. Seed phrases are encrypted locally and never leave your device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Chain" icon="layer-group">
    Support for Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, and Polygon from one wallet.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privacy-First" icon="eye-slash">
    Self-host on your own infrastructure. No tracking or analytics by default.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Source" icon="code-branch">
    MIT licensed. Audit the code, contribute features, or fork for your needs.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supported Networks

Walty supports these EVM-compatible chains:

- **Ethereum** - The original smart contract platform
- **Arbitrum** - Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum
- **Base** - Coinbase's Ethereum L2 network
- **Optimism** - Fast, stable, and scalable L2 blockchain
- **Polygon** - Ethereum sidechain with low fees and fast transactions

<Note>
All chains support native tokens (ETH, MATIC) and ERC-20 token standards.
</Note>
